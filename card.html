Capybara

This is the largest known rodent on the planet

  • Location: South and North America
  • Weight: 35-66kg
  • Lifespan: 8- 10 years
  • Predators: Jaguars, caimans, anacondas and pumas

Its remarkable size and abilities sanctify it from other rodents for many reasons. This rodent is very well adapted for land and water environments. It boasts a record of five minutes holding its breath underwater and has webbed feet for enhanced aquatic movement. This extraordinary guinea pig is also quite social and has been known to get along "swimmingly" with other species such as domestic dogs.
